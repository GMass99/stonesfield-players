Why We Do It

More Than Just a Drama Group

🎭

70+ Years of Theatre

Founded in 1953, we've produced everything from farce to Shakespeare, from pantomime to original drama — and we're still going strong.

⭐

Award-Winning

We've successfully entered local drama competitions, winning both junior and senior categories. Several of our young members have gone on to professional careers.

🤝

Everyone Welcome

Whether you want to act, direct, build sets, do lighting, or just help out — there's always room for new members. No experience needed.

Get Involved

We're always looking for new members — on stage and behind the scenes. Come and be part of our next production.
